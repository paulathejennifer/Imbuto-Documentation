How-To Guides

How to Register an Account

  1. Navigate to the registration page: /register
  2. Enter your personal details: name, email, phone number.
  3. Set a secure password.
  4. Click Submit.
  5. Confirm your email through the verification link sent.
  6. Log in with your new credentials.

How to Join as an Official

  1. Navigate to the Main section in our Imbuto website.
  2. Click Login or Signup.
  3. Enter your required details.
  4. If logging in, wait to be redirected to the official dashboard.
  5. Await confirmation notification.

If you face issues, check the Troubleshooting section.
